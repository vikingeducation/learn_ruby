Growth for Code

You're a wiz with growth marketing. We're geeks about code. We have much to discuss!

Here's the deal...

We're really good at teaching people to code. It's what we love and it's why almost all our time is spent building a great product around it. We'd like to get the message out about the program we're running and improve several key growth metrics but we need the bandwidth and expertise to do so. That's where you come in.

If you're an adaptive marketer who lives and breathes growth and user acquisition (particularly around content-based approaches) but want to level up your skills by learning to code, we've got an offer for you:

If you help us grow, we'll teach you to code

The details of this will be figured out case-by-case, but we run a highly valuable online program geared towards bringing advanced beginners to an employable level in full stack web development. These skills can put rocket boosters on your abilities going forward. This is a great opportunity whether you're hungry to learn everything or trying to stay ahead of the curve in digital marketing.

For consideration or more info, email your CV and why you're awesome to hello@vikingcodeschool.com.

More Details

What it looks like:

  • You'll initially help determine the high level growth strategy.
  • You'll then take the reins on implementing that strategy in the trenches. We can handle the technical writing.
  • Beyond implementing the core strategy, you will have the leeway you need to devise and run experiments.
  • This isn't an hour-a-week consulting gig -- we're looking for someone who will work with us directly on a part-time or even full-time basis. If the relationship works out beyond the skills-trade phase, we'll obviously be interested in continuing it on a more permanent (paid) level.
  • You would start ASAP. If this post is up, the position's open.

What we're looking for:

  • You are hungry to learn and succeed. While we prefer people with a proven track record, we also respect the hell out of hustle.
  • You understand the learn-to-code space and have a strongly technical bent.
  • You are legitimately fun to work with.
  • You write and communicate very well. You want to make meaningful changes in education.
  • Preference if you can pass our technical screen and participate full time in our web development program when it runs.
  • Preference for SF/Bay Area locals and those who can commit to full time (though remote is okay).

We've got a lot of appreciation for the difficult grind that is "growth" but we have a relevant product, strong referrals, and a wealth of content to work with. You can learn more about the program here, but note that it will shortly be changing in some meaningful and exciting ways.

Send your CV and a brief description of why you're awesome to hello@vikingcodeschool.com

If you know someone who might be interested, please share this with them!
